Build your factory.

Buy resources in the shop, process them in different ways such as crushing, pressing, mixing and washing.

Sell your products to buy more machines, add more automation and of course expand your factory!

Complete quests to unlock more items in the shop and to get more opportunities to expand your factory.

Your goal, make as much money as possible.
